About Mr Beans Café

At Mr Beans Café, we believe in creating more than just coffee. Our mission is to build a welcoming space for everyone in the community. Whether you're here for a peaceful moment, a productive work session, or a joyful meet-up with friends, we have something special waiting for you.

Founded in 2025 in the heart of Weston-super-Mare, we bring together warm service, quality ingredients, and a cozy atmosphere to create an unforgettable café experience.
